WhatsApp Privacy Plus

WhatsApp Privacy Plus is a Chrome extension that enhances your privacy on WhatsApp Web by applying blur effects to sensitive content like chats, contacts, and media previews.

It helps you use WhatsApp Web safely in public or shared spaces.

Features

  • Blur chats and contacts
  • Adjustable blur intensity
  • Hover to reveal content
  • Keyboard shortcut (Alt + X)
  • Lightweight and fast
